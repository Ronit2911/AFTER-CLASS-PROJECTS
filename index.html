About Us

Sri Chaitanya Techno School (SCTS) is committed to providing quality education with a focus on technology and innovation.

Founded in 2005, SCTS has grown into a leading institution where academics and extracurricular activities go hand in hand. Our mission is to foster creativity, critical thinking, and responsible citizenship among students.

Our Vision

To be a hub of excellence where students embrace technology and values to shape a better world.

Our Mission

Deliver holistic education through innovation, guidance, and a nurturing environment.
